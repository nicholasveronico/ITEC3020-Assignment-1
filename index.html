IPHONE 15 AND IPHONE 15 PRO

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The iPhone 15 series brings a whole new design featuring colour-infused back glass and a strong aluminum chassis. The beloved Dynamic Island provides a new way to interact with your notifications and background tasks. Its 6.1-inch display paired with the new 48MP sensors provide excellent clarity to your screen and cameras. New this year is the all- new charging port: USB-C. You can expect faster charging and transfer speeds with the updated port.

And for the iPhone 15 Pro series, you can expect a new processor -- the remarkable A17 Pro. Say goodbye to the mute switch and hello to the Action button, where you could customize it to do over 10 different actions in a click. Plus, with the third camera, you get up to 5x optical zoom, with excellent low-light shots. A new Titanium shell boasts a matte-glass back and incredible durability.
